About TireGo

TireGo is a mobile application designed to help drivers quickly connect with nearby tire repair shops and tow truck providers whenever they experience a roadside emergency such as a flat tire, tire damage, or vehicle breakdown. Using real-time GPS location, TireGo instantly finds the closest available service providers, allowing users to request assistance in just a few taps.

Features

📍 Live GPS

Find nearby tire repair services using your real-time location.

🚛 Tow Truck Requests

Request professional towing services directly from the application.

📱 Real-Time Tracking

Track your service request and provider status instantly.

🔐 Secure Login

Phone number authentication powered by Firebase Authentication.

⭐ Reviews

View ratings and reviews before choosing a service provider.

⚡ Fast Response

Designed to minimize waiting time during roadside emergencies.

Privacy

TireGo values user privacy and only collects the information necessary to provide location-based roadside assistance.
